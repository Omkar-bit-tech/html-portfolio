The Best Movies According to Omkar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

One piece

This is my favourite anime. It’s an epic adventure with great characters—fun, emotional, and full of heart.

Interstellar

Emotional and epic. The space scenes and music are incredible.

The Matrix

Classic sci-fi. Cool action and a mind-blowing concept.

Inception

Mind-bending and stylish. Loved the dream layers and action scenes.

Avatar: The Way of Water

The visuals are stunning. Loved exploring the underwater world.
